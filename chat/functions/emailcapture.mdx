---
title: 'E-Mail Capture'
description: 'E-Mail Capture in the Chat widget at submission'
---
The E-Mail Capture feature allows you to capture the user's e-mail address when they submit a chat. This is useful for following up with the user after the chat has ended. The e-mail address is stored in the chat transcript.

## Usage

When E-Mail Capture is enabled, the user will be prompted to enter their e-mail address when they submit a chat.

## Enable E-Mail Capture

To enable or manage E-Mail Capture, go to **Settings** > **Chat Widget** > **E-Mail Capture** and toggle the switch to **ON** or **OFF**.
