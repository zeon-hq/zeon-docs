---
title: 'Internal Notes'
description: 'Learn how to set internal notes for your chat widget conversations'
---
Internal notes are a great way to keep track of important information about your customers. You can use them to keep track of things like:

- Customer's order number
- Customer's email address
- Customer's phone number
- Customer's account number
- Customer's shipping address

## How to add an internal note

To add an internal note, click on the **Add internal note** button in the chat widget.
