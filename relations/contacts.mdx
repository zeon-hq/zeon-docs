---

Zeon relations module lets you manage your contacts. You can add, edit, and delete contacts from the admin panel. You can also add custom fields to your contacts.

## Adding a Contact

To add a contact, go to **Contacts** > **Add Contact**. You will see a form with the following fields:

- **First Name**: The first name of the contact.
- **Last Name**: The last name of the contact.
- **Email**: The email address of the contact.
- **Phone**: The phone number of the contact.
- **Company**: The company of the contact.
