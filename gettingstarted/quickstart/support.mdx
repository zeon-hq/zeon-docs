## Community

The best place to get support is the [Discord server](https://discord.gg/ZtPdPmu9xW). You can ask questions, get help, and chat with other community and Zeon users. Our team is also active on Discord and will be happy to help you. 

## Official Support

If you need to contact us directly, you can email us at [team@zeonhq.com](mailto:team@zeonhq.com). We will try to respond to your email as soon as possible.

## Live chat

You can also chat with us directly on our [website](https://zeonhq.com). Just click the chat icon in the bottom right corner of the page. We will try to respond to your message as soon as possible.
