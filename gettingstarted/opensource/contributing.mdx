# Contributing

Zeon is an open and modular approach to online productivity solutions. We welcome contributions of all kinds, from bug reports to pull requests and everything in between. We also welcome contributions to our documentation, which is hosted on GitHub. If you have any questions, please feel free to reach out to us on [Discord](https://discord.gg/xG2nbu5CMr). We're happy to help!
